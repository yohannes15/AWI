What We Can Do?

ARCHITECTURAL MILLWORK AND WOODWORK

Through our extensive experience of managing woodwork and millwork projects, we have gained a trustworthy reputation as an industry leader in all forms of Woodworking and Millwork. AWI is known for exceptional woodwork and millwork design for homes and businesses in the tri-state area.

We approach all our works with a special love for perfection and satisfaction for our customers. Our millwork specialites range very wide as you can see on this page.

CUSTOM CABINETRY AND INSTALLATION

Our Cabinetry services include economical kitchen cabinets design and installation, Cabinet Restoration, Custom office cabinets, semi-custom kitchen remodeling, commerical cabinets installation, kitchen remodeling nyc and more custom cabinerty services.

CUSTOM STAIRCASE CONSTRUCTION

We provide extensive guidance, research, experience and craftsmanship when building and constructing a staircase for our customers. American Wood Installers is the leader of stair contractors in New York City and its surrounding areas.

We build custom stairs of any shape, form and size. If quality is what you are looking for, American Wood Installers is the definite choice. Our wooden stairs and stair railing installation services are unriveled in our serviceable areas.

Outstanding Carpentry

Our carpentry contractors are all experienced and you will get the most for your buck when contracting us for any form of carpentry. Most of all, our customers come first, so we do a lot of planning, advising and communicating to make sure you are 100% satisfied.

Custom Closet Installation and Custom Design Closets

American Wood Installers are experts in custom closet installation. We take into account what our customer wants, needs and communicates to mastermind and create the cusotom closet he/she had in mind. The installation is always prompt and professional.

Guardrail Construction

Custom Shelving and Installation

At AWI, our Custom Shelving and Installation services are prompt and efficient. We are 100% customer-oriented, as a result, most of our customers are ecstatic about our home and commerical shelving services.

Our custom made union wood is also the best wood for all types of shelving including rack shelving and closet shelves.
