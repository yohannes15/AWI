How To Choose A Custom Cabinet Contractor in New York or Tri State Area [Residential or Commercial]

Did you just move into your new house or apartment in New York and are you looking for a company that can provide you with exceptional custom cabinetry skills to completely renovate your kitchen or your closet space ?

Or are you a building manager for a office or workspace and are you looking for a company that can provide you with commercial custom office cabinets to maximize your office space and efficiency?

Either way, you have come to the right place to learn about how choose the right custom cabinet contractor in New York whether that is for residential or commercial efforts.

Downfalls of Choosing A Low-Quality Cabinet Installation Service

If you are moving into a new home and you are expecting to live there for a significant time, it is important that you hire the best high quality cabinet contractor near your area.

Choosing a company that does custom cabinets in a low-quality fashion is the fastest way to get problems in the future that will end up costing you more than a high quality cabinet contractor service.

Let me show you two examples that show the benefits of choosing a high quality custom cabinet service over a stock-based or low-quality custom cabinet contractor.

Let’s imagine Josh has a family of five, with three kids and he recently bought a house in Queens or Manhattan (I know he must be rich right :)) He moved into his house and saw an empty kitchen and wanted to hire a custom kitchen remodeling contractor in NY. He needs to answer the following questions first:

  • How long am I planning to stay in this house?
  • Am I looking for a cheap option or a reasonably priced and high quality service?
  • How modern do I want the kitchen remodeling?
  • Have I had a history of kitchen cabinets getting scratched up or falling apart?

    • The biggest determinant of whether or not to hire a high quality premium custom kitchen remodeling contractor is whether or not you are planning to stay in that house/apartment/condo for a decent amount of time.

    The longer you are planning to stay at a single location (mostly in the case of families), it is to your advantage to hire high quality custom cabinet contractors. Even though the prices might be higher than for example IKEA stock cabinets, in the long term, there will be less technical problems as well as quality problems.

    So in the case of Josh and his family, the best option for them is to hire a high quality custom kitchen remodeling service in Manhattan or Queens, wherever they are located.

    In the case of kitchen cabinets, it is highly important that you don’t jump on the cheaper and lower-quality route as costs of repairing and maintaining low quality cabinets can rise much higher than high quality custom cabinets in the long-run.

    If you answered yes to the first question and any of the other four questions, it is advised that you hire a custom cabinet contractor in New York rather than buying stock options or even worse, low quality custom cabinets.

    3 Amazing Benefits of Hiring The Right Custom Cabinetry NY Contractor

    1) Providing you a personal and unique option

    Most people looking for custom cabinet services are looking for kitchen cabinets, and you already know how much premium, modern and custom kitchen cabinets look better than stock or low quality options.

    The number one benefit that a high quality custom cabinet contractor provides you with is the ability to customize, specify and create unique designs and goals for your kitchen, instead of choosing generic and pre-built options such as IKEA or even worse, custom kitchen cabinet contractors that don’t take your ideas and listen to you, and instead, create their vision of a beautiful kitchen over your vision.

    2) Efficiency and Durability

    If you are a office manager and are looking for a custom office cabinet installer or contactor in NY, a custom cabinet contractor service provides you with unlimited specificity, efficiency and durability that stock options won’t even come close to.

    Office cabinets go through hell with the amount of people using them carelessly and continuously every single moment. As a result, it is important that your priority as a office/building manager is to hire high quality custom office cabinet contractors to ensure the durability of your cabinets and the efficiency of the office space.

    3) High Quality Craftsmanship and Materials

    Most, if not all, high quality custom cabinet contractors use high quality wood and woodwork for their cabinets.

    How To Make The Choice Out of The Vast Number of Custom Cabinet Contractors in NY

    Step 1: Think of a time where you saw amazing custom cabinet construction/installation at offices/commercial buildings or residential homes.

  • Go to that building manager and ask around on where he hired the contractor that did that outstanding job [In the Case of Office Cabinets]
  • If you know someone who has outstanding kitchen cabinets, ask them who they contracted. This is the simplest way for finding a potential suitor for your custom cabinet goals and will yield you, most of the times, great results.

    • Step 2: Do Research Online on Custom Cabinet Installers Near You

    More than 90% of people in the US look for options online before hiring a contractor. Don’t be a part of 10% that is misinformed or under-informed. Most companies have their website online, and you can see their “About Us”, “Portfolio”... pages to get an accurate description of their business.

    For example, if you are in Long Island and you are looking for a kitchen remodeling and cabinet service:

  • Just go on to Google and search for Long Island Kitchen Remodeling and Cabinet Installation Services and you will get hundreds of results and companies providing that service.
  • The first pages usually are ads, so be aware. Usually Google ranks pages based on satisfaction and how much people stay on a page, but you can buy ads and appear as the first choice if you have the money. I would advise checking the companies and websites that have naturally gotten into the first page of Google, instead of just choosing the ones with ads. [No hate towards ads; just informing you on something most people don’t know about]
  • Take a look at the focus of their services. Most companies have a specific focus, either residential home cabinet installation or commercial cabinet installations. Look for their focus and contract them if it fits your goals.

    • Step 3: Understand that High Quality Custom Cabinetry in NYC is really not that expensive.

    If you are staying at a particular residence or office for a long time, it is important that you put a significant investment in your cabinets whether they are kitchen cabinets or office cabinets.

    Costs of repairing and maintaining low quality cabinets are, as I said before, in the long-run more expensive than installing high quality premium cabinets in the first place.

    I hope we have provided you with the answers to questions you might have had on hiring a custom cabinet contractor in New York.
