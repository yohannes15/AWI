About AWI

Since it's foundation in 1995, American Wood Installers has grown into a quality and sought-after architectural millwork company with custom millwork solutions in the areas surrounding New York (New Jeresey, Long Island...)

With its quality craftsmanship and its exceptional woodwork and millwork services for residences and businesses, American Wood Installers has become a premium woodworking company that offers services such as

  • Italian interior design, custom office cabinets
  • Custom kitchen cabinets, custom closet installation, custom wood stairs
  • Hardwood floor installation
  • Roof deck construction, commerical wood shelving
  • Porch construction, new door installation

Our Mission

When contracting American Wood Installers, you get the highest quality of finest custom woodwork in New York and its surrounding areas.

Our primary goal is 100% customer satifisfaction and to make sure there is a smile on your face before we finish a millwork project.

When we tackle a millwork project you can be absouletly certain that it will be delivered right on time, within the set budget limits and at the pinnacle of high quality woodwork. We get all our liabilities insured, including third-party liabilities, thus indemnifying our clients against all risks.

We pride ourselves in providing professionalism, exemplery service and outstanding quality in all our millwork services.
