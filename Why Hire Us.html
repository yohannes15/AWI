Why Hire AWI? The Benefits of Contracting A Unionized Millwork Service Contractor

American Wood Installers is a member of the Association of Wall-Ceiling and Carpentry Industries in New York. The president of American Wood Installers, Edward Heberer, is a board member for that union. Now you might be wondering why should I contract a union millwork service provider, when I can hire a non-union architectural millwork contractor for less money?

IF QUALITY IS AN IMPORTANT ISSUE FOR YOUR BUILDING/HOME, AWI GUARANTEES YOU PREMIUM QUALITY THAT WILL PROVIDE YOU WITH NO LATER-ON COSTS THAT ADD UP AS A RESULT OF LACK OF QUALITY THAT IS SOMETIMES ASSOCIATED WITH CONTRACTORS THAT AREN'T UNIONIZED

Becoming a union contractor is much more than just some legal documents and some name. Unions help in building connections that allow us to provide a premium service at a respectable price. By being in a union, AWI has access to thousands of craftful, professional and safety-conscious workers that can help your millwork goals to become a reality. By contracting us, you are guaranteed of high quality and high skilled workers. There is no worry of inadequacy as compared to hiring non-union contractors. All of our workers in our union have gotten specialized training and skill sets.

We not only provide high skilled workers, but we also provide an efficient team of workers that can do the job within the time frame. We deliver our services within the time frame agreed upon and only extend the period only in rare occasions, and that usually is because we want to provide you with an excellent quality service. Our workers are also heavily trained in safety, health and job-site hazards and as a result, you can be sure that accidents only happen in the rarest of times and that everyone's health including our team is protected with skill.
